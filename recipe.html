Messroom Chilli

Messroom Chilli is a spicy, flavorful dish that brings together the warmth of fresh chillies and rich spices. It's perfect for those who love a bit of heat in their meals. This dish is hearty and makes a great main course or a satisfying side.

Ingredients

Ingredient Quantity Preparation Time
Chilli Peppers 200 grams 5 minutes
Onions 1 large, chopped 3 minutes
Garlic 3 cloves, minced 2 minutes
Tomatoes 2, diced 5 minutes
Spices (Cumin, Coriander, etc.) 1 tsp each 2 minutes
Salt To taste 1 minute

Instructions

1. Heat a pan over medium heat and add a bit of oil. Saut√© the onions until they are golden brown.

2. Add the garlic and cook for another minute until fragrant.

3. Toss in the diced tomatoes and cook until they start to break down into a sauce.

4. Add the chilli peppers, cumin, coriander, and salt. Stir well to coat the chillies with the spices.

5. Cover the pan and let the dish simmer on low heat for 15-20 minutes, stirring occasionally.

6. Serve hot with rice or bread.
